About Me

I am a PhD researcher at IISER Kolkata working in the field of environmental chemistry. My research focuses on understanding how emerging pollutants (nanoplastics, pharmaceuticals) interact with minerals in natural environments, influencing their transport, transformation, and ultimate fate.

Currently, I am synthesizing sustainable, eco-friendly materials to mitigate pollutant exposure in aquatic systems.

Beyond research, I enjoy sports and reading stories.
